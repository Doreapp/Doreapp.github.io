Bouguenais, France

SIREHNA is a dynamic and innovative company specializing in engineering solutions for the maritime industry. With a team of highly skilled engineers and technicians, SIREHNA provides a wide range of services, including design, development, and manufacturing of high-tech maritime equipment and systems. The company is known for its innovative solutions, which are designed to improve the efficiency, safety, and sustainability of maritime operations.
With a strong commitment to customer satisfaction and a culture of collaboration and teamwork, SIREHNA is a trusted partner to the maritime industry. The company's innovative solutions, combined with its expertise and customer-focused approach, make it a leader in its field.
Small size business (100+ employee), subsidary of Naval Group.

  • Software engineer intern

    From February 1, 2022 to June 30, 2022

    During my internship at SIREHNA, I was tasked with building a simulation and data management environment for the hydrodynamic engineers of the company. The goal of the internship was to improve their working processes and automate hydrodynamics simulations. In this context, I developed a full environment, containing the following components: A large storage system based on MinIO, which provided a robust and scalable storage solution for the simulation data. A user and permission management web platform based on Girder, which enabled secure access and management of the simulation data. An automated workflow to run CFD simulations and asynchronously return the results and logs, based on Gitlab. This streamlined the simulation process and allowed for efficient data management. This internship allowed me to gain experience in a professional setting and develop my skills in software development, data management and automation.
